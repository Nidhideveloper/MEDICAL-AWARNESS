Cancer is a disease characteried by excessive cell growth which have potential to spead to the other parts of the body as well. Common Signs or Symptoms of Cancer are possibliy lumps, prolonged cough, abnormal weight loss, abnormal bleeding, and many more. 		PANCREATITIS

Pancreatitis is a disease characterised by an inflammation in the pancreas. It may occur suddenly, or over a period of days, or even over span of years. It can be caused because of gallstone going into the pancreas, or by chronic or heavy use of alcohols. Symptoms include pain in the upper abdomain, nausea and vomiting. 		ACL INJURY

ACL tear injury or a torn in anterior cruciate ligament in the knee, which is identified by the pop sound which comes when injured. ACL is a tissue which connects thighbone with the shinbone, present in knee. Mostly the ACL injury occurs in player of football, tennis, etc, and in women because of the low muscule strength. Symptoms of ACL tear include knee swelling, and sever pain.
